Robot Project 2018

In one of my classes, a team of three was required to build a working model for an autonomous undersea cable-laying robot (shown on the right). The objective was for the robot to take a cable from a spool and take it to a beacon, where it had to be attached. In my group, I was the team leader and main programmer. This gave me plenty of experience in programming microcontrollers. The entire project used VEX microcontrollers, and was coded in a variant of C, which is were I learned to write complex programs in C. Our project did so well that we received an A for the project and were invited to the Uvic Robotics competition, which we did compete in. The whole project was designed and developed from January to March 2018. This project inspired me to start a robot project of my own, which I am currently working on with a colleague. I look forward to the future experience that I hope to gain in this field.
